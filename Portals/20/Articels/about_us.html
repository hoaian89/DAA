Welcome to Vietnam Association for Fluid Mechanics

The Vietnam Association for Fluid Mechanics or VAFM was founded on mm 19xx. VAFM is a non-profit organization and a member of the Vietnam Society of Mechanics or VSM.
The goal of the VAFM is to promote and support scientific, educational and cultural activities with regard to research, development, application of fluid mechanics; to provide a platform to interactively discuss and exchange ideas; and to disseminate their achievements among the general public. a
The VAFM publishes the annual Vietnam Proceedings of Fluid Mechanics or VPFM as well as sponsors the annual Vietnam Conference on Fluid Mechanics or VCFM.
Major VAFM activities include organizing and sponsoring conferences, symposia, and workshops for all members, publishing books, proceedings, and reports, and awarding grants, scholarships, and other honors.
On behalf of VAFM Executive Committee, we would like to have the honor of greeting you with open arms and hope that you will have an opportunity to joint our operations and to enjoy with us.
